Awards and achievements - Dr Abbas Mistry

In this ever—evolving field of modern medicine, we endeavor to not only remain up-to-date with the breakthroughs and advances of this field but to also contribute to it.

  • Awarded Dr. Neena Premchand Daheja's First prize for 1982 - 1983 for outstanding work in Community Medicine at Urban Health Centre, Malavani.
  • Awarded Bombay University Scholarship while registered for M.S. and M.Ch. Examination.
  • Outstanding Achievement & Excellence Award in Medicine for the year 1996 - by Giants group of Byculla, Mumbai.
  • He was presented the Testimonial and Meritorius Service Medal for 1999 in appreciation of Dedicated and Outstanding Services on 27.02.2000 by St. John Ambulance Brigade, No. 3,  Maharashtra District.
  • He was awarded the President's Medal as a Corps Surgeon with Saifee Ambulance on 27.02.2000 by Anjuman e Shiate Ali.
  • Appointed as a Faculty Member for Basic Course on Micro Surgical Techniques  -  Organized by Indian Society for Reconstructive Micro Surgery at Ethicon Institute of  Surgical Education, in Mumbai.
  • This course was started in August 1998 and is being conducted on a bimonthly basis.  In each course 12 students from various branches in Medicine are taught basic Microsurgical Techniques.
  • Invited Faculty Member for ONCORECON - 2010 Cadaveric Dissection Workshop organized by Department of Plastic Surgery, Tata Memorial Hospital & K. E. M. Hospital, Mumbai in 2010.
  • Invited Faculty Member for APSI - ETHICON Accredited Instructional Course in Head & Neck Reconstruction held in Bombay Hospital on 21st and 22nd November, 2013

  • At K. E. M. Hospital under the Guidance & along with Dr. S. R. Tambwekar & Dr. Edwin Turkoff from Vienna, Austria, evaluated & studied Intra-Operative Electro Diagnostic Method using Spinal/cortical Stimulation to determine the exact site of lesion in Peripherals Nerves in Leprosy.
  • At K. E. M. Hospital along with Dr. S. R. Tambwekar studied the Possibility of Autologous Skin Culture in Management of Extensive Burns & Giant Nevi.
  • Co-author of a Publication “ Multiple Osteolytic Skull defects-Band Pattern-Significance of Detection” in American Journal of Roentgenology, July 1991.

  • First author of a Publication “ Tibial Cancellous Bone Grafting in the Management of Alveolar Clefts” in Journal of Cleft Lip and Craniofacial Anomalies, Vol. 5/Issue 2/July-December 2018.
  • First author of a Publication “Outcome of Surgical Management of Sacrococcygeal Pilonidal Sinus Disease with Rotation Flap in 52 Patients-A Retrospective Study” in Indian Journal of Plastic Surgeons, 2021 Apr;54(2):163-167.
  • Co author of "Comparison of Conventional Lefort I advancement, anterior maxillary segmental distraction & distraction osteogenesis of maxilla for surgical management of cleft maxillary hypoplasia. A systematic review." published in the Indian Journal of Cleft Lip And Craniofacial Anomalies, Vol 8/Issue 1, Year 2021.
