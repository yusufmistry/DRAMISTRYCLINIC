About your Doctor

Dr Yusuf Mistry

MDS, FHNO

Head and Neck Cancer Surgeon

After completing his MDS in Oral & Maxillofacial Surgery, from MGM Hospital, he has been awarded a clinical fellowship in Head & Neck Cancer Surgery from HCG Hospital, Ahmedabad, Gujarat.
He specializes in cancer of the oral cavity as well as all other kinds of cancer surgeries pertaining to the head and neck region. He has a varied clinical experiences from MGM Hospital, Prince Aly Khan Hospital, Tata Hospital & Saifee Hospital including the opportunity to work under Dr Sultan Pradhan, Dr Abbas Mistry & Dr Srivalli Natarajan.
Currently, he is an honorary consultant Head and Neck Oncosurgeon at Saifee Hospital and also holds an academic position of a lecturer at MGM Dental College, Navi Mumbai.
