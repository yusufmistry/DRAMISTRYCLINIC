Giving back to the Community

Dr Abbas Mistry

Dr Abbas Mistry has always been at the forefront when it came to community services.

  • He has been a part of the Annual Plastic Surgery Camp - Dharamshala, organised by the the Lions Club from 1993 to 1998 where every year a team of doctors from Mumbai under the guidance of dr KS Guleria would operate approximately 100 free surgeries.
  • He was invited by the General Consulate of Yemen to give lectures and perform surgeries at the Aden Hospital, Yemen. He had performed many complicated reconstructive surgeries and given educational lectures to the local doctors and staff at Aden Hospital during his visit.
  • He was instrumental in starting the Department of Cleft Lip & Palate at Sumandeep Vidyapeth University, Waghodia, Baroda. He has performed many cleft surgeries at that centre for free.
  • He has been a part of umpteen number of medical camps, cleft missions & training programs.

Few of his important contributions to society are.

  • Attended the 7th, 9th, 11th & 12th Annual Plastic Surgery camps under the Leadership of Dr. K. S. Goleria in 1993, 1995, 1997 & 1998 at Dharamshala, organized by The Lions Club of Dharamshala, Himachal Pradesh. Approximate 100 Plastic Surgery operations are performed every year, free of charge.
  • Invited by the Consulate General of the REPUBLIC OF YEMEN to give Lectures & Demonstrate Operations in YEMEN, which was attended in OCT-95 at ADEN Hospital. (YEMEN).
  • Organized a Plastic Surgery Camp at Pachora (Dist. Jalgaon) in March '98 with the help of Burhani English Medium School and Indian Medical Association - branch Pachora. Approximate 100 patients were examined and 15 Major Reconstructive Surgeries performed, free of charge.
  • Organized a Plastic Surgery Camp in Sumandeep Vidyapeeth University, Waghodia, Baroda on 20th & 21st February, 2009. 18 Major Reconstructive Surgeries were performed on patients from tribal area of Kavant free of charge. Subsequently helped them in starting a Department on Cleft Care.
  • Was a part of Multi-specialty camp in Taherabad, M. P., India, where more than 2000 patients were treated between 13th and 14th June, 2009.
  • Participated in the Medical Check Up Camp held in Bordi, Maharashtra on 21.12.1997 by St. John Ambulance Brigade, No. 3, Maharashtra District.
  • Participated in Officer's Training Camp held in Khandala, Maharashtra on 5th and 6th January, 2002 by St. John Ambulance Brigade, No. 3, Maharashtra District.
